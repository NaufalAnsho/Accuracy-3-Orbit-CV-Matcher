4. Engineering

Mechanical Engineer

Are you a problem-solver with a strong grasp of mechanical systems? We're looking for a Mechanical Engineer to join our team and contribute to the design and development of innovative products.

Mechanical Engineer Responsibilities:

- Designing and analyzing mechanical systems and components.
- Conducting feasibility studies and testing.
- Collaborating with cross-functional teams on product development.
- Creating detailed engineering plans and specifications.
- Overseeing manufacturing processes to ensure product quality.
- Staying up-to-date with industry trends and technological advancements.

Mechanical Engineer Requirements:

- Bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering or a related field.
- Previous experience in mechanical engineering is an advantage.
- Proficiency in CAD software and engineering analysis tools.
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.
