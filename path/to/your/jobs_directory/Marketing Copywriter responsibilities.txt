Job brief
We are looking for a Marketing Copywriter to create compelling and effective copy for marketing materials.
You will be responsible for working closely with the marketing and creative teams to understand the company’s brand and messaging, and using data and analytics to measure the effectiveness of your writing.
The ideal candidate will have experience in marketing copywriting, strong writing and editing skills, and a deep understanding of the company’s target audience and goals.

Responsibilities
Create compelling and effective copy for marketing materials, such as website content, emails, and social media posts
Work closely with the marketing and creative teams to understand the company’s brand and messaging, and create copy that aligns with the brand
Use data and analytics to measure the effectiveness of writing, and make adjustments as needed
Collaborate with other departments, such as product and sales, to create cohesive marketing materials
Stay up-to-date on industry trends and best practices in marketing copywriting
Conduct market and customer research to understand the target audience and create relevant and engaging copy
Edit and proofread marketing materials to ensure accuracy and consistency
Measure and report on the success of marketing materials, using metrics such as website traffic, email opens, and conversions
Identify and resolve challenges and issues within the marketing copywriting process
Attend industry events and conferences to build relationships and promote the company
Provide training and support to team members on marketing copywriting best practices and tools
Contribute to the development and continuous improvement of the company’s marketing copywriting process

Requirements and skills
2-3 years of experience in marketing copywriting or a related field
Strong writing and editing skills, with a portfolio of writing samples
Familiarity with data analysis and reporting tools, such as Google Analytics
Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively with a team
Knowledge of the company’s target audience and goals
Ability to conduct market and customer research to create relevant and engaging copy
Strong problem-solving and decision-making skills
Time management and organizational skills
Flexibility and adaptability to change
A desire to learn and grow in the marketing copywriting field
Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communications, or a related field
Fluency in a second language is a plus
